Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate a medication schedule?

Go to the Dashboard page, enter your prescription instructions in the input box, and click "Generate Schedule". The system will automatically organize medicines into Morning, Afternoon, Evening, and Night based on your instructions.

Can I upload a prescription image?

Yes, absolutely. Navigate to the Upload Prescription page and upload a clear photo or scanned image of your prescription. Our AI system will extract the medicine names and dosages automatically for you.

How do reminders work?

You can configure reminder times from the Reminders page. Prescripto will send you notifications at the specific times you've set, ensuring you never miss a dose. Make sure your browser notifications are enabled.

What if the schedule is incorrect?

Our system is highly accurate, but if you notice an error, you can edit the instructions and regenerate the schedule. If you still have trouble, please use the contact form on this page to reach our team.
